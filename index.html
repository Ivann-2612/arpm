Key points


• A cointegrated process is a stationary process (46.26)-(46.27) that is a linear combination of non-stationary processes. • Cointegration is the essence of statistical arbitrage [W]. • Cointegration can be detected by principal component analysis (36.57) of the ill-defined) estimated covariance of the non- stationary processes.
